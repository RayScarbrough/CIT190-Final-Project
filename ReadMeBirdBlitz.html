Clueless Games

Purpose of the Game

Control a turtle while avoiding birds diving down. The player earns points for successfully dodging each bird. The goal is to achieve the highest score possible. However, if you're hit by a bird, the game ends. The challenge lies in reacting quickly to the birds movements and maneuvering the turtle to avoid collisions, testing the player's reflexes and agility.

How to Play

1. Start the Game: Click start game.

2. Once the game starts move the turtle left and right by using A and D on your keyboard.

3. The birds will fall from the top of the screen. Dodge as many as possible to gain points. The goal is to survive as long as possible.

4. End of Game: If you are hit by a bird the game will end!

Current Problems or Issues
