Clueless Games

Purpose of the Game

Snake is a classic arcade game where players control a snake that grows in length as it consumes food items. The objective is to guide the snake to eat as much food as possible without colliding with itself or the game boundaries.

How to Play

1. Start the Game: Load the game and control the snake using the W A S D keys on your keyboard.

2. Eating Food: Direct the snake to eat food items that appear on the screen. Each food item increases the length of the snake.

3. Avoiding Collisions: Ensure the snake does not collide with itself or the game boundaries. Collisions end the game.

4. Scoring Points: Score points for each food item eaten. The game may feature additional scoring mechanics or bonuses.

Current Problems or Issues

-The controls sometimes feel clunky when you attempt to turn back into yourself. The game is programmed right now to not allow the player to turn directly back into themselves. This causes the player to feel as if the controls are not responding, even though it is to help the player.
