Clueless Games

Purpose of the Game

Blackjack provides players with an experience of the classic casino card game. The player competes against the dealer to reach a hand value closer to 21 without exceeding it.

How to Play

1. Start the Game: Place your bet. The minimum bet is $50.

2. Dealing Cards: Once the bet is placed, you and the dealer will each receive two cards. In blackjack, the card values are as follows: Numbered cards (2 through 10) are worth their face value. Face cards (Jack, Queen, and King) are each worth 10 points. Aces can be worth either 1 or 11 points, depending on which value benefits the player's hand the most without exceeding 21.

3. Playing Your Hand: Choose to 'Hit' to take another card or 'Stand' to hold your total and end your turn.

4. End of Game: If your hand exceeds 21 points, you bust and lose the game. If you stand, the dealer will play their hand. The best hand wins the game.
